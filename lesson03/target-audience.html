The target audience is those that want up to date information on the local weather in Rexburg and local news bulletins

A couple of example personas that could benefit from this site would be someone who is very outdoors and relies on accurate weather forcasts to determine whether or not it is a good time to go outside to do some activites.

Another example of a persona would be a person that has dogs that like to go outside a lot and the person could use the news bulletins to find any new local parks.

Scenarios that could apply to this could be:

  1. Will it rain today so I can put my plants inside?
  2. When will the first snow happen? So I know to switch my tires ahead of time
  3. Will it be sunny today so I can take my kids to the park?
  4. When will the local fair happen?
  5. What are some upcoming commuity activites I can attend?
